Personalized Trinket Box:

These little boxes are made to order, but have an intrinsic vintage style. Upcycled Altoids tins get stripped of coatings, hand painted with a resist in a design of your choice, and then etched to raise the design and give it texture. Afterwards they're plated in either copper or silver using an ancient Roman technique, followed by hand burnishing to impart a unique, distinctive look. Add gun-black or gold-leaf accents for a special finishing touch.
